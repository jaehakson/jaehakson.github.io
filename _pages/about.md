I am an evolutionary biologist that takes an integrative approach of genetics, molecular biology, a wide array of state-of-the-art genomic and transcriptomic techniques. My long-term research goal is to understand how the non-coding and coding sequences influence genomic architectures and complexity. To reach this goal, I am working on 1) the transposons-host intragenomic conflicts and 2) non-canonical trajectories of sex chromosome evolution. 

I am currently an Postdoctoral Associate in [Ellison lab](https://ellisonlab.website){:target="_blank"} at Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey. I did my first Postdoc in [Aksoy lab](https://medicine.yale.edu/lab/aksoy/){:target="_blank"} at Yale School of Public Health. I did my Ph.D in [Meisel research group](http://nsmn1.uh.edu/rpmeisel/){:target="_blank"} at the University of Houston.
